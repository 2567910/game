The Game will Start in a Second... SpaceRocks is a game where you play as an Astroid and your only goal is to destroy the Planet you are heading for. On your way though Space you have to collect Rocks to getbigger to be able to destroy the Planets. But be careful, the Planets are trying to defend themselves by firing Rockets at you!
